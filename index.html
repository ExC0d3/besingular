    Genesis
    BeSingular was founded with a sole purpose to prepare the next generation for a tomorrow that is changing both, rapidly and drastically.

    It is promoted by Best Group , a diversified entity backed by entrepreneurs, who have over four decades of experience in manufacturing, child development, education and dealing with cutting edge technologies.
    Goals
    To create a platform for young minds to get inspired and look at technology as a way to solve global challenges.

    We aim to do this by introducing them to the impact exponential technologies can create and motivate them to embrace the knowledge for high achievement and creativity.
    Faculty
    BeSingular’s content has been carefully customized to impart innovative education on technology with a high level of relatability. Our global team has been exceptionally inspired to help in creating the curriculum to empower each child to be better citizens.

    BeSingular’s trainers are upskilled by an in-house developed training regimen which has a right mix of technology and empathy, to make every child stand out and unique in their abilities.

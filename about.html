Our Organisation

BeSingular

BeSingular is a unique learning platform to educate and empower the next generation to create a real and responsible impact in the world by working with the technology of the future.

The innovative and well researched curriculum imparts skill sets based on Exponential Technologies which will benefit them in every aspect of their lives.

What We Offer

Our unique approach towards explaining Exponential Technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, AR/VR, Machine Learning, Coding is our USP. When taught, these tools help the children at BeSingular to participate in solving unique problems and opens up the world of infinite possibilities.

We engage children through practical activities which ensures that they grasp the concept and fosters their development of problem solving, creative and critical thinking skills.

The curriculum is curated by global experts who have been a pioneer in their field of expertise. The faculty that runs the programme is trained with a specially curated BeSingular training module which is an interesting mix of empathy and tech.


The Founding

BeSingular is an initiative started by our founder Nitesh Jain who is passionate about innovation and technology. His keen observation, study and research on the growth of future technologies ignited the idea to break the traditional thinking of relating tech with one set of children.

He desires to address the impending need of empowering the next generation from various age groups and fields, with the tools to help create the world of abundance.

Our Brand Promise

BeSingular is a learning platform to skill the next generation, to create real and responsible impact by working with the technologies of the future .

We aim to inspire and create exceptional learning experiences by being mindful and actionable to the latest trends, both in industry & technology, to provide skills for future relevance.

Why We are the Best

The most important aspect of our foray into technology academia is our resources, passion and know-how of the industry. We are the pioneers in adapting the future innovative technologies into our businesses and have the first-hand experience of the benefits of these technologies.

Our organisation and the management team are associated with some of the world’s leading companies and universities that help us in bringing the required edge at BeSingular, making us distinctive and exclusive.

Nitesh Jain - Founder
Managing Director of a diversified The Best Group, Nitesh is passionate about empowering the next generation to help solve global challenges and create a better world. He believes young minds adept to use exponential technologies can create an immense impact by contributing to changing times.

He is a mentor at Smart Mobility Accelerator program at Tel Aviv University, Israel, a Founder at Ashoka University, Ambassador at Singularity University, Delhi Chapter, India and Board Member at Harvard Alumni Entrepreneurs in India.
Swati Jain - Founder & Director
She is a multi-tasker, problem-solver, a hands-on compassionate leader and has pioneered the Preschool and Child Day Care business. She is known for her work on creating women empowerment and helping to ease their lives to better manage their professional and personal responsibilities.

A Psychology major from Jesus and Mary College, Delhi University, she holds an MBA degree from Fore School of Management and is a mother of three.
Shweta Khanna - COO
A faculty with Undergrad and Post Grad programs at Delhi University, University of Bradford UK and Exchange programs at National University, Singapore she fits right into the role of Chief Operating Officer.

She has worked with CBSE, NSDC and NSQF in more than 14 states across 6000 + schools and has been associated with corporate training – design and delivery across domains. She has extensive experience in designing, implementing and scaling new training interventions in the education sector.

An avid reader and traveller, she is passionate about Early Childcare and believes in continuous learning and zest to celebrate life in every given moment. She has also been associated with PWC HR and The World Bank.
Siddhartha Jaitly - CTO
As a visiting student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA, Siddhartha has been involved in research work. He has also held the post of Research Associate at Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi where he has worked on various research projects including KUKA robots series.

He has also assisted and led various research and industrial projects for startups, educational institutes and companies in India. Since 2011 he has been actively involved in teaching the young minds of India about engineering, robotics and programming. His enthusiasm is infectious and it is safe to say that he is an engineer by profession and an educator at heart.
